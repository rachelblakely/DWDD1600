Home

Rachel is a fresh graphic designer and artist who loves to create beautiful, colorful works. While Minnesota-born-and-raised, she's pleased to be based out of Utah Valley (for almost ten years, now)! She's been creating art and designs since she could first hold a crayon in her little hands and take great enjoyment out of turning her passions into a living. Have a seat and please explore her hand-coded site--we're so glad that you're here!

Her Philosophy

Rachel has a passion and taste for good design like no other. Seeking to honor her intuition, she effortlessley blends her personal style into any need, noting trends without dishonoring the core of a brand identity.

Some Things to Know

To ensure full transparency and earnest honesty, relevant definitions are laid out below. In the case of unsurety or confusion, please reach out for any necessary further clarifications.

Educator
A educator is a licensed professional with an institution-certified degree to teach in a formal capacity. While any individual who has knowledge to share can be a teacher, this definition is what may be inferred in the context of this site.
Creator
A creator is an individual employed in a creative field or recreationally pursuing creative passions, including but not limited to one or more of the following: visual arts, fine arts, graphic design, audio/visual design, pedagogy, illustration, photography, experience design, traditional crafts, and various modern forms of content creation.
